Frequently Asked

What documents are needed for a new GST registration?
Typical requirements include PAN card, Aadhaar, Proof of Business Address (Electricity bill/Rent deed), and Bank details. We handle the entire digital filing process for you.
How often should my business undergo an internal audit?
While statutory audits are annual, we recommend quarterly internal reviews for scaling businesses to maintain financial health and catch discrepancies early.
Do you provide representation for income tax appeals?
Yes, Mr. Nazir personally handles high-stakes tax litigation and appeals representation before the ITAT and other authorities.
